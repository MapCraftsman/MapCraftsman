# 💫 About Me:
I am Arun Kumar K C, a Senior Geospatial Software Engineer with 7+ years of experience in building enterprise-grade GIS, GeoAI, remote sensing, and spatial data engineering solutions. My work spans utilities, smart cities, agriculture, land administration, environmental resilience, and digital twin ecosystems.<br><br>My core strength lies in combining deep geospatial domain expertise with software engineering and scalable data systems. I specialize in ArcGIS Enterprise, FME, PostGIS, GeoServer, Python, JavaScript, Docker, and cloud-ready geospatial pipelines, enabling organizations to transform complex spatial data into reliable operational intelligence.<br><br>I am particularly passionate about GeoAI, remote sensing machine learning, land administration systems, smart infrastructure, and climate resilience technologies. My background in both research-driven GIS innovation and enterprise software delivery helps me bridge the gap between advanced spatial science and production-ready systems.
